Business Case

The Fastest ROI Path in the Playbook

Vendor negotiations at discount retailers have always been asymmetric. Suppliers arrive at every session with polished market data, category studies, and optimistic ROI forecasts for their promotional asks. Buyers arrive with last quarter's results, a gut feel from years of relationship, and a spreadsheet assembled the night before.

Agentic AI fundamentally rebalances this dynamic. The vendor intelligence agent generates a live, comprehensive negotiation brief before every session: commodity cost indices, the supplier's actual promotional ROI (not what they claim), head-to-head benchmarks against alternative suppliers, and specific carrot-and-stick scripts tailored to the category and vendor history.

Quarterly vendor check-ins transform into rolling monthly growth councils. Instead of reviewing the past, teams use agentic AI to surface new partnership and media opportunities — forward-looking, commercially aggressive, and joint in nature. This is where the fastest return is realized: margin capture typically appears within the first contract renewal cycle.

Agentic AI Role

The Vendor Intelligence Agent

What the Agent Does Autonomously

Before every vendor session, the agent auto-generates a complete negotiation brief: live commodity cost trends (so buyers know when a supplier cost increase claim is inflated), the actual incremental lift from that supplier's last three promotions, head-to-head benchmarks comparing their terms against alternative suppliers, and specific negotiating scripts — each with an associated carrot (growth opportunity) and stick (alternative sourcing threat). Post-negotiation, the agent tracks vendor commitments and alerts the buyer when performance falls short, building a continuous record of accountability that compounds leverage over time.

Agent Workflow: The Rolling Vendor Growth Council
01
Pre-Session Brief Generation
48 hours before each vendor meeting, the agent assembles a full intelligence package: cost indices, promo ROI actuals, competitive benchmarks, and negotiation scripts.
02
Live Benchmarking During Talks
Agents surface real-time supplier cost trends and margin forecasts during the session. Buyers never accept a vendor's numbers without an immediate data counterpoint.
03
Opportunity Surfacing
Agent identifies new media, joint promotional, and product development opportunities based on category performance data and shopper basket trends.
04
Commitment Tracking
Post-session, agent logs all agreed terms and monitors vendor execution. Automatic alerts when funding, volume, or fill-rate commitments fall behind target.
05
Leverage Compounding
Every cycle builds a richer performance record. Buyers enter each successive negotiation with more data, more leverage, and more specific evidence — the agent gets more powerful over time.
