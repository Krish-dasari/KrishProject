What Is Agentic AI?

Beyond Automation.
Beyond Gen AI.

Agentic AI systems are autonomous, goal-driven AI agents that can plan, act, and learn — continuously. Unlike static automation tools or one-shot gen AI models, agentic systems run 24/7, get better with every cycle, and coordinate across functions from vendor to shelf.

McKinsey research found 71% of merchants say AI tools have had limited or no effect on their business. The reason isn't the technology — it's the integration. Agentic AI overcomes fragmented data, static decision cycles, and siloed systems.
