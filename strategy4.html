Business Case

Promotions Are the Biggest Margin Leak in Discount Retail

Promotions are both the lifeblood and the biggest source of margin destruction in discount retail. Most promotional spend is allocated by habit — last year's playbook, vendor pressure, and a TPR calendar that hasn't been fundamentally rethought in years. The result: a significant portion of every promotional dollar funds events that generate zero incremental lift.

Today's promo process is reactive by design. An energy drink promotion launches on Monday. It underperforms by Wednesday. The merchant finds out Friday when someone builds a deck. By the time a course-correction decision is made, the promotional window is over and the margin is gone.

The Promo ROI Engine inverts this entirely. Agents detect underperformance on day two, diagnose the root cause (wrong store cluster, wrong price point, wrong medium), and recommend a budget reallocation with projected P&L impact — all before the buyer's first meeting on day three. The promotional budget works harder because every dollar is continuously optimized, not just planned once and forgotten.

Agentic AI Role

How the Promo ROI Agent Works

What the Agent Does Autonomously

When an energy drink promotion begins underperforming on day two — exactly the McKinsey "Natalie" scenario — the agent flags it in the morning signal brief with a complete root-cause diagnosis and a reallocation recommendation with simulated P&L impact. All before the buyer's first meeting. The agent also continuously A/B tests display bundles across store clusters and simulates new bundle-and-media promotions that link product bundles with targeted digital placements. Crucially, the agent optimizes for item-level margin contribution, not just units moved or engagement metrics — avoiding the "click-through mirage" pitfall McKinsey identifies as a common adoption failure.

Agent Workflow: Real-Time Promo Optimization
01
Pre-Launch Simulation
Before any promo event executes, agent simulates expected lift, margin impact, and cannibalization risk across store clusters. Buyers approve with full visibility, not blind faith.
02
Live Performance Monitoring
Agent tracks promo performance against expected lift in real time — daily, not weekly. Detects underperformance on day two with root-cause diagnosis included.
03
Mid-Flight Budget Reallocation
Underperforming promo budget redirected to high-ROI slots immediately upon merchant approval. No waiting for the weekly deck — the window is preserved, not wasted.
04
Bundle & Media A/B Testing
Agents design and execute bundle-and-media combination tests across store clusters, surfacing which pairings drive incremental basket and which are merely cannibalizing existing purchases.
05
Post-Event Learning Feed
Every promo event outcome feeds back into the agent's ROI model. Each new cycle starts with sharper predictions — the system compounds in accuracy over time.
